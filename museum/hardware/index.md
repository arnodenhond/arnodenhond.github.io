## Raspberry Pi 5

```plaintext
CPU: 2.4 GHz ARM Cortex-A76

```

[Arduino Nano](https://docs.arduino.cc/hardware/nano/)

```plaintext
CPU: 16 MHz ATmega328P
RAM: 32 KB Flash, 2 KB SRAM, 1 KB EEPROM
Modules: RTC DS1307, Mod4 Max7219 8x32LED
```
