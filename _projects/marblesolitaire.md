# Classic peg solitaire puzzle game

![](https://gitlab.com/arnodenhond/marblesolitaire/-/raw/master/play-assets/listings/en-US/graphics/icon/marblesolitairebigicon.png align="center")

Train your brain with Marble Solitaire, the classic peg solitaire puzzle game now on mobile! Also known as Peg Solitaire or Solo Noble, this timeless board game challenges you to clear all the marbles by jumping one over another until only one remains.

With simple rules but deep strategy, Marble Solitaire is easy to pick up yet difficult to master. Perfect for puzzle lovers of all ages!

Features:
* Classic peg solitaire gameplay
* Smooth and intuitive controls
* Beautiful, simple design
* Unlimited undo & restart options
* Play anytime, anywhere â€“ no Wi-Fi needed

Can you leave just one marble on the board? Put your logic and problem-solving skills to the test in Marble Solitaire today!
